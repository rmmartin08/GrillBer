Grillber

GrillBer is exactly like it sounds: Uber for grills! Let me set the scene. You live in a dense urban environment, in a studio apartment on the 20th floor of an apartment building, but you want to put on a cookout! You don't have the space or the budget to buy a high quality grill, but your local park doesn't provide public access grills for your cookout. What do you do?

That's where GrillBer comes in! GrillBer is a rental and delivery service for grills! Grill renters can put up the specs and descriptions for their grills, and users can rent them for specific amounts of time! The grill owner will drop off the grill at the designated time and location, and then pick it back up when the user is finished!

FAQs

Do I need to clean the grill?: Yes, you should totally clean the grill after use with the provided tools, additional fees may apply for grills that have not been cleaned.

Do you supply the gas/charcoal/woodchips?: All propane grills will have the tank included, any charcoal or wood grills will require the GrillBer user to supply thier own fuel source. These items are offered on the app for additional purchase.

Do I need to provide anything for the person that drops off/picks-up the grill?: This is optional, if you are willing you can tip (with monetarily within the app or food)

How and Why

How do I set up these services: Just download our simple app and once you set up your request the grill owner will drop off the grill at the specified time and location
